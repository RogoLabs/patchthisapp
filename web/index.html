Welcome

PatchThisApp helps organizations focus their patch management efforts by providing a curated, up-to-date list of high-priority CVEs. Our data is sourced from leading open-source intelligence platforms and refreshed hourly.

CISA KEV Catalog

Official list of known exploited vulnerabilities maintained by CISA.

Rapid7 Metasploit

Exploit modules for vulnerabilities, widely used by security professionals.

Nuclei Templates

Automated vulnerability scanning templates from Project Discovery.

EPSS

Exploit Prediction Scoring System for prioritizing patching based on risk.

Access the full, hourly-updated CSV database or contribute on GitHub.
